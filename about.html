Who's Jerma985?




Jerma turned into a catboy by the power of Bugelberry's magic pot

Jerma985 is a streamer that lives in Las Vegas, Nevada. Born in 1985, he primarily streams videogames, although occasionally hosts large events such as the "Jerma Dollhouse", where his life was controlled in a mock-up home by his twitch chat. Jeremy "Jerma985" Elbertson has made a large career and become largely popular on twitch. Jerma is associated with other streamers/content creators such as MoistCr1tikal and Ludwig. Jerma was last spotted on Ludwig's 'Chess Boxing' event.
